## Source Use Rules

- During-event sources may be used to simulate what could have been known at T1 or T2.
- Post-event investigation sources may be used for validation and diagnosis only.
- No source in this registry is a machine-readable flood extent.
- No source should be interpreted as confirming model-estimated damage.
