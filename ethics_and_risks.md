# Ethics and Risks

Disaster Impact Nowcaster is a decision-support and reproducibility tool. It must not be used as an autonomous emergency decision system.

## Non-Negotiable Limits

- Do not identify individual people affected by a disaster.
- Do not report confirmed damage unless validated data explicitly support that claim.
- Do not publish personally identifiable information.
- Do not expose sensitive locations when doing so could increase risk.
- Do not treat priority scores as official allocation decisions.

## Required Interpretation

Outputs should be described as:

- estimated exposure;
- potentially affected infrastructure;
- not confirmed damage;
- requiring validation.

## Validation

Users should compare outputs with official reports, local knowledge, field assessments, and known data gaps before using them in any public or operational setting.
